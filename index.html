Why God4Life Exists

Not just clothing. A reminder.

God4Life was inspired by my foster care journey and what it feels like to go through hard seasons thinking you’re alone.

Looking back, I realized God was there the whole time. He had His hand on me even when I couldn’t see it, and He’s the reason I got through what I did.

This brand exists to help other people feel that same comfort and safety, knowing they have immediate access to God, and He’s with them always.
